Wishlist Website Plan

Site Name

Wishlist Website

Site Purpose

To provide a platform for users to discover, curate, and save interesting products they find online, without the pressure of immediate purchase.

Scenarios

  • I'm looking for a unique birthday gift for my friend who loves vintage watches. Can this site help me find something special?
  • I often browse online and find cool things I want to buy eventually, but I don't want to buy them right now. Can I save them somewhere on this site?
  • I want to find unique beads for my jewelry making hobby, can I find them here?

Color Scheme

Primary:Warm beige or cream (#F5F5DC)
Secondary: Muted teal or olive green (#808000 or #468499)
Accent: Burnt orange or rusty red (#A0522D)

Typography

Heading Font Montserrat
Body Font: Open Sans
